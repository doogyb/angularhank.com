angular hank

Angular Hank is first and foremost 4 multi-instrumentalist singer/songwriters in a shed making rock music together. Their debut album ‘Brand New Angle’ gives the listener Punk, Country, Rock and pop music throughout, with each writer showing a new angle to Hank. The band does not conform to traditional roles of rock, which states that there are frontmen and ‘backmen’. In fact they are often seen arguing over the mic at shows. Listeners are not just presented with 4 voices, but confronted by 4 personalities. The band produced and recorded the entirety of their debut album and mixed all but 3 tracks. This approach was taken to incite creativity and to channel the shed-energy which which makes Mathieu, Samuel, Ronan, Doc and inevitably the listener excited for their next show. With ‘Brand New Angle’ only making up a fraction of their catalogue the band gigged extensively through 2019, including being asked to open for some of their favorite Irish acts such as Squarehead, The Claque and Pillow Queens.
